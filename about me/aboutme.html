About Me

Hello! My name is Karen and I am a passionate photographer from Brazil now living in Seattle. Photography has always been my love and I enjoy capturing beautiful moments in people's lives.

With a keen eye for detail and a creative mind, I strive to create stunning images that tell stories and evoke emotions. Whether it's capturing the love between couples, the joy of maternity, or the essence of a portrait, I am dedicated to providing my clients with memorable photographs that they will cherish for a lifetime.

Feel free to explore my galleries to see some of my work. If you have any questions or would like to book a session, please don't hesitate to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
